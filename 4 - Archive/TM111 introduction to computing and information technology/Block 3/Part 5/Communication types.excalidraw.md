there are different ways of categorising communication types.
The one we will look at is a categorisation based on the presence
or not of the receiver at the time of the sending action. Like
many categorisations, the distinctions can get blurry. Overall we
make the distinction between synchronous and asynchronous communication.
The first type can be said to be real-time, the messages are
transient and it is in ways similar to a phone call. The second
is time-dependent, the messages are persisted to be seen by
the receiver later and are more similar to a forum.
There is no best option but it all depends on the needs we have
and the uses we are going to apply, also, the tools we use can
sometimes be applied to both types of communications ^bqURsTLA
